Description

CONCAVE SHAPE PILLOW: 

The edges of the Rise & Shine’s Baby pillows are slightly raised than the middle concave part to prevent Flat Head Syndrome.

This pillow helps in correcting the alignment of baby’s neck and spine by dispersing the pressure uniformly.

The overall thickness of the pillow is 1 inch, suitable for 0 – 12 months old infant who needs neck and head support.

 

MEMORY COMFORT® FOAM: 

Made up of imported materials these memory foam pillows bring you the most comfortable sleep. As the name suggests Memory foam has the ability to remember the original shape, it slowly returns back to it after being compressed. The pillow is made up of 100% pure Visco-Elastic memory foam.

 

TEMPERATURE SENSITIVE MATERIAL: 

It allows our body heat to activate the molding process so that it gives us personalized fit. The climate control features also does not allow the pillow to become hard in winters.

 

MULTIPURPOSE PILLOW– 

The Rise and Shine baby pillow can be used in bed, stroller, cradles, prams, cots and chair for baby’s comfort.

 

SPECIAL FEATURES:

  • Greater air flow & Refreshing Sleep
  • Better Support and Sleep- This pillow provides optimum comfort and support to new born babies.
  • Retains original shape after each use, Unlike Polyfill/Cotton pillows our pillows will not sag.
  • Total Value for Money

 

INNER COVER: 

We provide our pillows with two layers of Cover. The basic being a super soft premium printed fabric material to protect the pillow.

 

PREMIUM SOFT OUTER COVER: 

The super soft premium printed fabric as our Outer Cover for the Pillows is gentle on baby’s skin. It is Antiallergenic, eco-friendly and Easy to Care. The pillow comes with a removable cover which is easy to remove and wash.

 

WE ASSURE: 

Our Pillows are made up of 100% filler-free foam. We have tested our pillows in lab and have a strict quality check before it is dispatched. Our pillows are of high density foam so be assured that it will be your companion for long. We provide One year limited warranty. Please check our Warranty section.
