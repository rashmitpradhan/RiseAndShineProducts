Safety & Care

Q) Are the Pillows Waterproof?

Our pillows are not waterproof. We provide our pillows with two layers of premium cover to protect the inner foam. We prescribe that you take care to guard the Pillows from water or any fluid spills. It is also recommended to use the pillow covers provided with the bedsheet, it would add further protection to the Pillows.

Q) How do I clean my Pillows?

We spend around 7-8 hours sleeping on our pillows, so it is highly recommended to take care of the hygiene of the Pillow. We should always use the Pillow covers provided with the bedsheet and that should be washed every 7 Days. You can also remove the outer cover of the Pillow and spot clean the cover with mild detergent, Use a soft, damp and clean cloth using small circular motions to clean. Remember to dry the cover before putting it back on the pillow. Please Do not Machine wash the cover. For the Pillows you can keep it in the open air/ Sun to remove any smell if present.

Q) What is Memory Comfort® Foam?

As the name suggests Memory foam has the ability to remember the original shape, it slowly returns back to it after being compressed. The pillow is made up of 100% pure Visco-elastic memory foam.

 

Q) What is Laytex Comfort® Foam?

LAYTEX COMFORT® FOAM: If you prefer soft pillows then our Laytex Comfort® pillows are the perfect choice for you. Super Soft foam with latex feel is similar to natural rubber in terms of bounce and faster response time, the Pillow Remains cool because of the cooling technology which the pillow has. The Laytex Comfort® Foam also has few advantages over Natural Rubber like the pillows will never form knots, create lumps or collapse after sometime. 

 

Q) What is Gel Memory Comfort® Foam?

Rise & Shine Gel Memory Pillows have GEL Infused technology to keep the pillow cool while using. It maintains the temperature of the pillow by releasing the excessive heat generated by the human body during sleep. As the name suggests Memory foam has the ability to remember the original shape, it slowly returns back to it after being compressed. The pillow is made up of 100% pure Visco-elastic memory foam.
